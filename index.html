About Namma Kudla

Mangalore, affectionately known as "Kudla" in the local Tulu language, is a vibrant coastal city in Karnataka, India. Nestled between the Arabian Sea and the Western Ghats, this port city is renowned for its pristine beaches, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality.

From ancient temples and churches to spicy coastal cuisine and thriving trade history, Kudla offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity. Experience the charm of this beautiful city through our curated travel guides and local insights.
